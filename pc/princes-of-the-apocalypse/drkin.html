Abjure Enemy ✦ Action

Present your holy symbol and speak a prayer of denunciation. Choose a creature that you can see within 60 feet. It must make a Wisdom saving throw unless it is immune to being frightened. Fiends and undead have disadvantage.

On a failed save, the creature is frightned. While frightened, its speed is 0 and it can't benefit from any bonus to speed. On a successful save, the creature's speed is halved. The effect lasts for 1 minute or until the creature takes damage.

Vow of Enmity ✦ Bonus Action

You utter a vow of enmity against a creature you can see with 10 feet. You gain advantage on attack rolls against the creature for 1 minute or until it drops to 0 hit points or falls unconscious.
