Magical Tinkering

Action. With artisan's tools in hand, touch a Tiny nonmagical object to give it one of the following magical properties:

  • It sheds light in a 5-ft. radius and dim light for another 5 ft.
  • When tapped by a creature, the object emits a recorded message of no more than 6 seconds that can be heard 10 ft. away.
  • It continuously emits your choice of odor or nonverbal sound, perceptible up to 10 ft. away.
  • A static visual appears on one of its surfaces — a picture, up to 25 words of text, lines and shapes, or a mixtures of these elements.
This effect lasts until you use your action to touch the object and end it. You can affect up to 3 objects at one time. If you try to exceed this limit, the oldest effect immediately ends.

Background: Soldier

Etch achieved the rank of Spear in a hobgoblin legion, and served as quartermaster. War has been his life as long as he can remember. As a youth, he trained hard, studied the use of weapons and armor, and learned basic survival techniques, including how to stay alive on the battlefield.

Personality Trait

I'm always polite and respectful.

Personality Trait

I'm full of inspiring and cautionary tales from my military experience relevant to almost every combat situation.

Ideal: Might

In life as in war, the stronger force wins.

Bond

Flaw
