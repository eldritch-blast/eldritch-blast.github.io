Rage

Bonus Action. You enter a rage that lasts for one minute, until you are knocked unconscious, or until you end your turn without attacking a hostile creature or taking damage since your last turn.

While raging, if you aren't wearing armor, you gain the following benefits:

  • +2 melee weapon damage
  • Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage
You recover expended uses of Rage when you finish a long rest.
