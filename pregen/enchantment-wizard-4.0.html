Hypnotic Gaze

Action. One creature that you can see within 5 feet is charmed by you if it fails a Wis saving throw (use your spell save DC). The charmed creature's speed drops to 0, and the creature incapacitated and visibly dazed.

On subsequent turns, you can use your action to extent the duration until the end of your next turn. The effect ends if you move more than 5 feet away, if the creature can neither see nor hear you, or if it takes damage.

Once the effect ends or the creature makes its saving throw, you cannot use this feature on that creature again until you finish a long rest.
