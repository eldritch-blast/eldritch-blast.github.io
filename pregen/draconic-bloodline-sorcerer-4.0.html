Draconic Ancestry

You have a red dragon ancestor. Whenever you make a Charisma check when interacting with dragons, double your proficieny bonus if it applies.

Draconic Resilience

When not wearing armor, your AC is 13 + Dex. You gain +1 maximum hp per sorcerer level.

Elemental Adept

Spells you cast ignore fire resistance. When you roll damage for a spell that deals fire damage, you can treat any 1 on a damage die as a 2.

Metamagic
You can alter a spell you cast in one of the following ways.
Heightened Spell ✦ 3 sorcery points

Give one target disadvantage on its first saving throw against a spell you cast that requires a saving throw.

Twinned Spell ✦ sorcery point cost = spell level

Target a second creature with a spell that normally targets one creature and doesn't have a range of self. The second target must be in range. Costs 1 sorcery point for cantrips.

Background: Courtier

In your earlier days, you were a high-ranking official in the government bureaucracy of the city-state in which you grew up. After your draconic heritage manifested itself, you left that life behind to develop your new found sorcerous power. You still have carefully cultivated relationships with the movers and shakers back home, and the skills you honed as a courtier will serve you in good stead as an adventurer.

Personality Trait

I believe that anything worth doing is worth doing right. I can't help it—I'm a perfectionist.

Ideal: Community
