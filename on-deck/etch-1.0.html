Darkvision

You can see in dim light as though it were normal light, and darkness as though it were dim light, up to 60 ft. away.

Magical Tinkering

Action. You can use tinker's tools or other artisan's tools to touch a Tiny nonmagical object and give it one of the following magical properties:

  • Shed bright light in a 5-ft. radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet.
  • Whenever tapped by a creature, emit a recorded message up to 6 seconds long that can be heard within 10 ft.
  • Continuously emit an odor or nonverbal sound, perceptible within 10 ft.
  • Show a picture, up to 25 words of text, lines and shapes, or other static visual effect on one of its surfaces.

The chosen property lasts indefinitely. As an action, you can touch it and end the effect early.

You can bestow magic on up to 3 objects, but only one property per object. If you exceed this maximum, the oldest effect immediately ends.

Saving Face

If you miss with an attack roll or fail an ability check or saving throw, you can gain a bonus to the roll equal to the number of allies you can see within 30 feet of you (max +5).

Background: Soldier

War has been your life for as long as you care to remember. You trained as a youth, studied the use of weapons and armor, learning basic survival techniques, including how to stay alive on the battlefield. You were party of a mercenary company that employed a large number of goblinoids.

Specialty: Quartermaster

Military Rank

You were a lieutenant in your mercenary company. Soldiers loyal to your former military organization still recognize your authority and influence, and they defer to you if they are of a lower rank. You can invoke your rank to exert influence over other soldiers and requisition simple equipment or horses for temporary use. You can also usually gain access to friendly military encampments and fortresses where your rank is recognized.
