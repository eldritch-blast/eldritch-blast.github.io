Character Creation

The following rules supercede or supplement the rules for step-by-step character creation in chapter one of the Player's Handbook. The house rules here are designed to make character creation possible with no dice rolls (and hence no DM oversight), as well as to provide flexibility for players to choose character concepts that play against stereotype. Each section is marked as either an option or a rule; players who aren't interested in additional flexibility can safely ignore those marked as options.

Option: List of Races and Subraces

When selecting a race and subrace, you may choose from any of the options shown in the Race and Subrace Options table.
